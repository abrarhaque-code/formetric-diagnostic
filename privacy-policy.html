Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Information We Collect

When you use our Financial Diagnostic tool, we collect the following information:

  • Contact Information: Your name, email address, and company name
  • Business Information: Industry focus, business stage, revenue range, and other business metrics you provide
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our diagnostic tool, including completion time and responses
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate your personalized financial health analysis
  • Send you your detailed diagnostic report via email
  • Provide educational financial guidance and insights
  • Improve our diagnostic algorithms and user experience
  • Send you relevant educational content (with your consent)
  • Offer consultation opportunities (only if you express interest)

3. Information Sharing

We never sell your personal information. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With service providers who help us operate our platform (email delivery, analytics)
  • In anonymized, aggregated form for industry research

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with reputable providers

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Rectification: Ask us to correct any inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured format
  • Withdraw consent: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@formetric.com

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Diagnostic responses: 3 years or until you request deletion
  • Email communications: Until you unsubscribe
  • Analytics data: 2 years in aggregated, anonymized form

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and analyze usage patterns:

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality and diagnostic progress saving
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics 4 to understand user behavior and improve our service
  • Performance Cookies: Track website performance and identify technical issues
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and customize your experience

Third-Party Analytics:

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymized data about how visitors use our website. This includes:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Geographic location (country/city level only)
  • Device and browser information
  • Conversion events (diagnostic starts, completions, email submissions)

Google's use of this data is governed by their Privacy Policy. You can opt-out of Google Analytics using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookie Control Options:

  • Browser settings: Disable or delete cookies through your browser preferences
  • Google Analytics: Use the opt-out browser add-on mentioned above
  • Do Not Track: We honor "Do Not Track" browser signals where technically feasible

8. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with trusted third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase: Database and backend services for secure data storage
  • Resend: Email delivery service for sending diagnostic reports
  • Google Analytics 4: Website analytics and user behavior tracking
  • Cloud hosting providers: Secure infrastructure for platform operation

Each service processes data according to their respective privacy policies. We ensure all third-party services meet our security and privacy standards.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through our platform.

11. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have additional privacy rights under regulations like GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California):

GDPR Rights (EU Residents):

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of data processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

CCPA Rights (California Residents):

  • Right to Know: Information about categories and specific pieces of personal information collected
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service and pricing regardless of privacy choices

How to Exercise Your Rights:

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@formetric.com

We will respond within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA) and may require identity verification for security purposes.

12. Contact Information

For questions about this privacy policy or our data practices:

Email: privacy@formetric.com

Response time: We aim to respond within 48 hours

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Formetric, we believe privacy is fundamental to building trust. We collect only the information necessary to provide you with valuable financial insights and always prioritize transparency in how we handle your data.

Your trust drives our commitment to responsible data practices.
