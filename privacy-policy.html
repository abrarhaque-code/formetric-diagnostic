Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Information We Collect

When you use our Financial Diagnostic tool, we collect the following information:

  • Contact Information: Your name, email address, and company name
  • Business Information: Industry focus, business stage, revenue range, and other business metrics you provide
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our diagnostic tool, including completion time and responses
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate your personalized financial health analysis
  • Send you your detailed diagnostic report via email
  • Provide industry benchmarking and insights
  • Improve our diagnostic algorithms and user experience
  • Send you relevant educational content (with your consent)
  • Offer consultation opportunities (only if you express interest)

3. Information Sharing

We never sell your personal information. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With service providers who help us operate our platform (email delivery, analytics)
  • In anonymized, aggregated form for industry research

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with reputable providers

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Rectification: Ask us to correct any inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured format
  • Withdraw consent: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@formetric.com

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Diagnostic responses: 3 years or until you request deletion
  • Email communications: Until you unsubscribe
  • Analytics data: 2 years in aggregated, anonymized form

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your diagnostic progress
  • Analyze website usage and performance
  • Provide personalized content

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

8. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with trusted third-party services:

  • Email delivery: For sending your diagnostic reports
  • Analytics: To understand user behavior and improve our service
  • Cloud hosting: For secure data storage and platform operation

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through our platform.

11. Contact Information

For questions about this privacy policy or our data practices:

Email: privacy@formetric.com

Response time: We aim to respond within 48 hours

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Formetric, we believe privacy is fundamental to building trust. We collect only the information necessary to provide you with valuable financial insights and always prioritize transparency in how we handle your data.

Your trust drives our commitment to responsible data practices.
